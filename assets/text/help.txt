The game board consists of a 6x6 grid, and there are several blocks placed on the board
in different positions.
The red block is the one you need to move out of the board, and it is placed in a horizontal
position at the center of the board.
The other blocks are placed in either a horizontal or vertical position, and they are
f different sizes.
To move a block, you can only slide it in a horizontal or vertical direction.
You cannot rotate or lift a block off the board.
Your objective is to slide the blocks in a way that you can move the red block out of the 
board by sliding it through the exit at the right side of the board.To win the game, you
need to move the red block out of the board in the fewest number of moves possible.
